Flabbergasted

Posted 5/06/15

OMG! You won't even believe what happened today. I was driving over to my co-worker's house when the strangest thing happened. I was pulled over by the police but when I got closer I realized that it was Elon Musk. He told me that I won a free 10 week coding course that he taught. He gave me a flight ticket to his estate and told me to meet him in a few weeks when he called. I just stared at him blanky as he drove away flabbergasted by what just happened.
